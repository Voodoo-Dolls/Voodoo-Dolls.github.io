Commando

Also known as the ‘Enabler Perk’, the Commando is one of the precision perks available in Killing Floor 2. Commandos excel in killing trash and they can handle 1-2 larges if they have an adequate amount of space between them.

Commando is probably one of the easiest perks to get into, but one of the hardest to master.

Pros

  • Can extend ‘Zed Time’

Cons

  • Slow movement speed

Weapons

Commandos usually specialize in the Assualt Rifles weapon class. These weapons offer great trash control and decent large killing potential.
