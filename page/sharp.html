Sharpshooter

Sharpshooter is one of the precision perks offered in Killing Floor 2. Sharpshooters excel greatly in killing larges and they can hold their own when it comes to trash.

+ Pros

  • One of the highest damaging Perks in the game
  • Only perk in the game that can kill 2 fleshpounds without reloading
  • Their lvl. 25 skill can help trigger Zedtime with every headshot
  • Very little recoil
  • Very high weapon switch speed
  • Cryogrenades offers huge CC ability

- Cons

  • Suffers from long reloads
  • Slow Movement
  • Constantly in a crouched position
  • Slow firerate during Zedtime
  • Has to be really accurate

Playstyle

Sharpshooter's in general wanna be aiming for the head in general; due to their damage bonus and Zed time activation chance. However, many CD sharpshooters fall in the range of two playstlyes: Big Zed Hunter or Headclicker. Both of these playstyles typically use the same loadout (M14 or FAL with a SPX) but, utilize a certain weapon more than the other. A good sharpshooter should be to swtich between these playstyles depending on the situation.

Big Zed Hunter

The Big Zed Hunter as the name suggests, their primary goal is to huntdown big priority targets such as Scrakes, Fleshpounds, and Husks. Big Zed hunters typically wield a SPX since it decap any mediums, but also take out Fleshpounds before they start moving (great for far range takedowns). Great style to use when laned with a trash killer.

Headclicker

The Headclicker playstyle is the more common and useful playstyle (for the most part). They primarily wield the M14 or FAL and get headshots on anything. Big major benefit of this playstyle is that it has a huge increase in headshots compared to the Big Zed Hunter. This allows zed time to trigger more often, creating opportunities for the team. The playstyle is flexible in any situation. Whether its' a solo lane or not, the M14 or FAL can handle trash and larges very effectively.

Tips & Tricks

  • Freeze into double FP takedown

    One of the strongest attacks you can make with sharpshooter is to freeze a group of fleshpounds, then quickly dispatch two fleshpounds with the SPX +2.

  • Rage Cancel into Freeze

    Don't suggest doing this often since people can potentially rerage the fleshpound and potentially kill you, but it is a handy trick to know.

  • Use Your Spares!

    One of the biggest disadvantages of sharpshooter would be their slow reload speeds with tube fed weapons. A way to be really agressive in a lane would be to simply pick up another spare to reload A.K.A 'V-Loading'.

  • Over-capping

    One neat trick you can do for Sharpshooter is over-capping! What overcapping does, it allows you to have the ammo benefits of Always Prepared, and the extra damage from Dead Eye. In order to preform the trick, you need to equip Always Prepared, fill up your weapons, drop all your weapons on the ground, then switch to dead eye. Once you pick up your weapons again, you'll notice that you will retain the ammo bonus but still have dead eye equipped.

    Note: Touching any ammo box will reset any ammo above the default.
