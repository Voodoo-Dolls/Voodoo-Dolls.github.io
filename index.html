Sharpshooter

Sharpshooter is one of the precision perks offered in Killing Floor 2. Sharpshooters excel greatly in killing larges and they can hold their own when it comes to trash.

+ Pros

  • One of the highest damaging Perks in the game
  • Only perk in the game that can kill 2 fleshpounds without reloading
  • Their lvl. 25 skill can help trigger Zedtime with every headshot
  • Very little recoil
  • Cryogrenades offers huge CC ability

- Cons

  • Suffers from long reloads
  • Slow Movement
  • Constantly in a crouched position
  • Slow firerate during Zedtime
  • Has to be really accurate

Strategy

In CD, Sharpshooters are typically large killers however, they can also excel in killing trash if the team needs it. Sharpshooter's really shine when laned with a teammate who can take care of trash for them; since they offer the highest dps outside of Zedtime, but slow reloads. Common Loadouts would be M14+1 or FAL with a SPX+2.

Tips & Tricks

Freeze into double FP takedown

One of the strongest attacks you can make with sharpshooter is to freeze a group of fleshpounds, then quickly dispatch two fleshpounds with the SPX +2.
